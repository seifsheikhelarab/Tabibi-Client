# Tabibi Client - Design Context

## Users

Patients booking appointments from home. People likely feeling some level of health concern or anxiety, needing reassurance and ease of use.

## Brand Personality

**warm and trustworthy** - The interface should feel approachable, calming, and reliable. Patients are vulnerable; the design should reassure them.

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Elegant and organic** - Not sterile or clinical. Soft, natural shapes, gentle transitions.
- **Theme**: Light mode only
- **Colors**: Primary blue (#5F6FFF) from existing implementation
- **Anti-references**: Avoid all AI slop patterns (gradient text, side-stripe borders, glassmorphism everywhere, generic card grids)

## Design Principles

1. **Soft reassurance** - Rounded corners, gentle shadows, warm undertones
2. **Clarity over cleverness** - Easy to understand, no confusing interactions
3. **Reduced anxiety** - Calm spacing, no cluttered interfaces
4. **Natural flow** - Organic curves, smooth transitions
5. **Trust through refinement** - Polished, intentional details that signal professionalism
